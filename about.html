About Me!

My name is Sam Yang, I'm a digital artist, living in Toronto. I have a Youtube channel, an Instagram account and a patrean account, where you'll find the most of my work. I share all my work on instagram, sharing advice and in process pieces on my Youtube, with more in-depth tutorials on my patreon. For a small to higher price with increased perks! My main focus is character design although I have various backgrounded artworks On Youtube I share the stages that take place in my pieces including sharing art advice :) Trying my best in setting an example for people eager to learn how to draw digitally or better their skills.
